Gus Grecco

I'm an Argentinian chef with 20+ years experience cooking worldwide , having a solid background in high-end and michelin rated restaurants, as well as a Private chef for UHNW clients between Madrid and New York.

My Cuisine & My Roots

"Seasonality, culture, and travel have shaped the chef I am today."

With my Argentinian-Italian-Spanish heritage, meat, pasta, and Mediterranean flavors are at the heart of my repertoire. Through my travels and training across South America and Europe, working alongside chefs from around the globe, I've built expertise in diverse cuisine with a strong emphasis in Latino Italian, Spanish, Thai and Chinese. Grounded in a deep respect for seasonality, my cooking is as flavorful as it is refined, warm and depth.

Tailored Culinary Experiences & Special health-oriented meals

Whether you need a private chef for executive meals, special occasions, or family-style dining, we are happy to arrange short-term bookings by the day or longer stays throughout Spain or across Europe. If your needs are more health-oriented, we have extensive experience designing menus for high performers, nutritional goals, and specific diets with excellent results.
