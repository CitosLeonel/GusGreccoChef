ABOUT ME

Argentinian accomplished chef, 15+ years experience with a solid background in high-end and michelin rated restaurants. Privacy oriented profile, strong creativity focus, both in high executive cooking and family style. Passion and excellence my keys for success.

MY CUISINE

“Seasonality culture and traveling have made the cook I am today.”

Due to my family heritage in Argentina and Italy, meat and pasta are beloved in my repertory; due to my trips and training in South America, Europe and Asia, I've achieved acknowledge in several cuisines as Peruvian, Italian, Spanish and Thai.

Based on total seasonality, Mediterranean driven and flavour packed like in Asia and South America, my cuisine is tasteful as it is delicate.
